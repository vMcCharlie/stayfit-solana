# Achievements Guide

This document outlines the achievements available in the application, their descriptions, and the criteria to unlock each tier.

## 1. Streak Master
**Folder:** `streaks_compressed`
**Subject:** Fire / Flame
**Description:** Consistency is the key to success. Keep your workout streak alive to fuel the fire!

*   **Locked (Grey):** The fire has not yet been lit. Start your first workout to begin.
*   **Bronze:** Ignite the spark by maintaining a **3-day streak**.
*   **Silver:** Fan the flames! Reach a **7-day streak**.
*   **Gold:** You're on fire! Achieve a **30-day streak**.
*   **Purple:** A legendary inferno! Sustain a **100-day streak**.

## 2. Weekend Warrior
**Folder:** `weekend_compressed`
**Subject:** Calendar / Date
**Description:** Don't let the weekend slow you down. Stay active even when the work week ends.

*   **Locked (Grey):** Weekends are for resting... or are they?
*   **Bronze:** Complete a workout on a **Saturday or Sunday**.
*   **Silver:** Complete workouts on **5 consecutive weekends**.
*   **Gold:** Make it a habit. Workout every weekend for **3 months**.
*   **Purple:** True dedication. Never miss a weekend workout for a **year**.
